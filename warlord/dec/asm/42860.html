42860: Flags: Game States #1
Holds a single byte, where each bit relates to an item or room state as follows:
Bit Relating To
0 Entering Lug's cavern
1 Entering the forest path
2 Picking up the rope
3 Entering the Vale of Cuchulainn
4 Picking up the urn
5 Entering the Inner courtyard
6 The ladder being placed against the platform
7 Entering the enchanted hills
Used mainly as a flag to ensure that points for game milestones aren't awarded more than once.
