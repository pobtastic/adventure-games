42861: Flags: Game States #2
Holds a single byte, where each bit relates to an item or room state as follows:
Bit Relating To
0 Entering the deep pool of water
1 Entering the bleak moorland
2 Entering the crystal cavern
3 Picking up the acorns
4 Placing the sword on the stone slab
5 Killing the Fomorian tribe
6 Entering the Vale of Rhia
7 N/A
Used mainly as a flag to ensure that points for game milestones aren't awarded more than once.
