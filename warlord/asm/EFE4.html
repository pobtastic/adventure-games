EFE4: Process: Take Cloak From Body
The player wants to take the cloak from the dead body, which is a bit creepy. Check which version of the cloak is in the current room.
Process_TakeCloakFromBody EFE4 LD A,$62 Call ValidateItemPresent with item 62: "The body of a young man,in a white cloak,trapped by charred fallen timbers".
EFE6 CALL ValidateItemPresent
Allow the player to take the cloak from the dead man who can't really complain about it.
EFE9 JP Z,Process_GetCloak Jump to Process_GetCloak if the version of the cloak is "The body of a young man,in a white cloak,trapped by charred fallen timbers".
Else respond that there is no cloak on the body.
Print "The body does not have a cloak.".
EFEC LD HL,$D308 HL=Messaging_BodyDoesNotHaveCloak.
EFEF JP PrintStringAndNewline_Duplicate Jump to PrintStringAndNewline_Duplicate.
