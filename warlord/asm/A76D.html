A76D: Flags: Game States #2
Holds a single byte, where each bit relates to an item or room state as follows:
Bit Relating To
00 Entering the deep pool of water
01 Entering the bleak moorland
02 Entering the crystal cavern
03 Picking up the acorns
04 Placing the sword on the stone slab
05 Killing the Fomorian tribe
06 Entering the Vale of Rhia
07 N/A
Used mainly as a flag to ensure that points for game milestones aren't awarded more than once.
