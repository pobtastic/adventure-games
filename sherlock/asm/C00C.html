C00C: Get Time Period
Used by the routines at CheckTimePeriod_Not6AM, CheckTimePeriod_NotMorningMidday, CheckTimePeriod_NotAfternoon, CheckTimePeriod_Not7PM, CheckTimePeriod_NotLateNightEvening and D078.
Handles calculating a time-period code for scheduling events.
Time Period Code Meaning
01 Late night/ evening (8 PM - 5 AM)
02 7 PM
03 Afternoon (1 PM - 6 PM)
04 Morning/ Midday (7 AM - 12 PM)
05 6 AM
Output
A Time period code
